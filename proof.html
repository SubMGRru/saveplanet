At the moment, five huge garbage islands have been discovered which drift in the waters of the Pacific, Atlantic and Indian Oceans and create huge risks for all their inhabitants. Some animals and fish take plastic micro-particles for phytoplankton and mistakenly eat them. The chicks of sea birds are attracted by larger plastic pieces of bright colors, young birds swallow them, condemning themselves to excruciating death. <img src="img/zhiteli-morskie.jpg" width="100%" height="100%"> This happens not only because of garbage. Power plants, for example, do the same harm. After all, waste water after leaving the power plants is quite warm, and some water residents die because of lack of adaptation to certain temperatures. Crude oil and petroleum products are the main source of the world ocean pollution. Oil gets into the water as a result of man-made disasters, tanker crashes and well drilling, but a lot of oil products are discharged by ordinary sea transport. Oil spills lead to the death of a huge number of marine animals, fish and birds, and in addition, they prevent normal heat exchange between layers of water. The Fund supports the creation of national marine parks and develops criteria for the designation of specially protected natural areas, develops sustainable fisheries, and conducts research to prove the harm to human activities. This work is carried out not out out out of pure love for science, but for an argumentative dialogue with politicians who have the power to permit or prohibit.
